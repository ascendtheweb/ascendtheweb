Free 5-Point Website Audit

1. Homepage Clarity

Your homepage is the digital front door to your business. We evaluate how quickly visitors understand what you do, who you serve, and what action they should take. Clear messaging, compelling headlines, and strategic visual hierarchy are essential for converting visitors into customers.

A strong homepage should answer the visitor's key questions within 3-5 seconds and guide them toward your primary conversion goal.

2. Mobile Load Speed

With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, your site's performance on smartphones and tablets is critical. We analyze loading times, image optimization, and mobile responsiveness to ensure your site provides a fast, smooth experience across all devices.

Slow mobile sites not only frustrate users but also hurt your search rankings and conversion rates.

3. SEO Basics

Search engine optimization ensures your website can be found by potential customers. We review your site's technical SEO foundation, including meta titles, descriptions, header structure, and content optimization to improve your visibility in search results.

Proper SEO implementation can dramatically increase your organic traffic and reduce your customer acquisition costs.

4. CTA Visibility

Call-to-action buttons and forms are the conversion engines of your website. We assess the placement, design, and messaging of your CTAs to ensure they're highly visible and compelling enough to drive visitors to take action.

Strategic CTA placement and design can increase conversion rates by 200% or more.

5. Security & UX

User experience and security build trust with your visitors. We evaluate your site's navigation, content organization, security certificates, and overall user journey to identify opportunities for improvement that will enhance credibility and user satisfaction.

A secure, user-friendly website not only protects your visitors but also signals professionalism and reliability.
